2023 Population Pyramid

The population pyramide represents a growing population with an increased proportion of young people and a broad working-age base. Vision Mission & Goals

VISION

By 2025, the Provincial Population Office (PPO), as the implementing arm for the Philippine Population and Development Program in the Province of Iloilo, will be dynamic, reliable, people-centered, technology-driven and culturally-sensitive population information resource agency in Region VI.

MISSION

To provide technical assistance and information needs to the Local Government Units (LGUs), partner agencies and stakeholders in carrying out measures relative to the integration of population and development, through the generation and utilization of reliable and updated data, and Implementation of population development programs and projects to uplift the quality of life of the Ilonggo population.

GOALS

  1. To generate and maintain an efficient and reliable data bank responsive to the data and information needs of the population development programs of the Province of Iloilo.
  2. To provide updated data and information to support program operations and development planning of LGUs, partner agencies, and other stakeholders.
  3. To undertake educational projects that promote people's participation and empowerment towards total human development in the context of our cultural heritage.
  4. To apply new technologies and processes that will effect more efficient and effective data generation, processing, and management.

Core Values

We, the constituents of the Provincial Population Office (PPO), commit ourselves to the following core values and endeavor to LIVE IT:

  • Love of Service: Love of fellowmen expressed through service beyond self is the overarching principle that guides all other actions and endeavors.
  • Innovation: We believe that the creation of new ideas and initiatives in the context of our cultural heritage is the noblest response to the challenge of change in our communities.
  • Vigor and Enthusiasm: Our most profound expression of our commitment to our vision, mission, and goals.
  • Excellence: Our efforts and actions will be guided by the highest standards of quality and excellence.
  • Integrity: We uphold the principle of truth and honesty, realizing that in our dealings, we are accountable to each other and to our clients, partners, and stakeholders.
  • Team Work: Given our diverse talent and skills, we will work together to achieve PPO goals.
